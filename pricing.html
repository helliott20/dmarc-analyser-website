Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free trial work?

Your 14-day free trial starts when you create an account. During the trial, you have full access to all features with no limits. No credit card is required to start. At the end of the trial, you can subscribe to continue using the service.

What counts as a domain?

Each unique domain you add for monitoring counts as one domain. Subdomains (like mail.example.com) are tracked under their parent domain and don't count separately. You're only charged for domains you actively monitor.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period. We don't offer refunds for partial months, but you won't be charged again after cancellation.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards through Stripe, including Visa, Mastercard, and American Express. All payments are processed securely.

Do you offer discounts for annual billing?

Not currently, but we're considering it. Our monthly pricing is already very competitive, and we prefer the flexibility of monthly billing for our customers.
